FAQs
1. What is deep fake detection?
Deepfakes (a portmanteau of "deep learning" and "fake") are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness.
2. Why Deepfake detection is important?
Deepfake al- gorithms can create fake images and videos that humans cannot distinguish them from authentic ones. The proposal of technologies that can automatically detect and assess the integrity of digital visual media is therefore indispensable.
3. How does Deepfake detection work?
